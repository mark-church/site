2018.12.02

Here are some of my favorite links around the internet.

Technology & Science

  • The Architecture of Open Source Applications - An amazingly detailed breakdown of how super common technologies work
  • MIT Technology Review - A look ahead at the scientific progress that will trickle down as world-changing technologies in the next decade
  • MartinFowler.com - Articles that taught me different software architecture design concepts such as backend design and decomposition to microservices
  • Kubelist - A regularly curated list of projects and events in the Kuberentes ecosystem
  • Julia Evans Blog - Assorted posts on Linux and networking that was actually a huge inspiration for this site
  • Hacker News - This site gets a lot of shit for being a Silicon Valley echo chamber but I still find interesting content here on every visit

Specific Technology Posts

  • Site Reliability Engineering by Google - The SRE bible for running distributed systems in production
  • Docker Internals - Deep dive in to what makes up containers and how the docker engine works
  • Operating Systems: Three Easy Pieces - A free digital textbook that taught me the fundamentals of operating systems
  • Packet Filtering in Linux by Purdue University - My iptables reference that I have to return to more often than I'm comfortable admitting
  • Awesome Docker - List of great Docker and Kubernetes resources

Product Management

  • Stratechery - A blog that perfectly blends technology with business strategy
  • The Product School YouTube - Large set of posts from PMs talking about their work

Life

  • Wait but Why
  • Mark Manson
  • No Zero Days - Motivational trick to help create or kill a habit

Money

  • Stockrow - A good site for filtering and comparing different equities
  • Bigger Pockets - Resources and community for Real Estate investing

Podcasts

  • Planet Money - Basically Freakonomics, but delivered weekly as bite sized stories
  • a16z Podcast - Podcast of Andreesen Horowitz focused on technology and business trends
